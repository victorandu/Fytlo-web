03 · forma

A try-on model built for actual bodies, not mannequins.

Most virtual try-on is a warped texture slapped onto a silhouette. Forma is a physics + ML hybrid trained on real drape: sleeve fall, shoulder seam angle, how a stiff denim behaves vs. a rayon blouse. It runs on-device. It knows the difference between a t-shirt and a knit.
